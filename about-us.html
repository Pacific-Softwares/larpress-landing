About larpress.Digital

A modern Laravel CMS & application starter kit built for developers.

Who We Are

larpress.Digital is a modern Laravel CMS & application starter kit designed to help developers and businesses build scalable, production-ready web applications faster.

It combines the power of Laravel, FilamentPHP, and Tailwind CSS into a clean, modular, and developer-friendly system.

  • ✅ Laravel 11.x foundation
  • ✅ FilamentPHP v4 admin panel
  • ✅ Modular & scalable architecture
  • ✅ Clean UI & developer experience

Our Mission

To simplify Laravel development by providing a powerful CMS and starter kit that reduces setup time and increases productivity without compromising quality.

Our Vision

To empower developers worldwide to focus on building great products instead of repeating the same boilerplate work.
